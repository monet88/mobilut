### Domain: product_strategy/lut_pricing_and_branding  
- **Catalog structure**: 200 curated LUT presets shipped together, leveraging licensed sources (G'MIC Film-Luts [MIT], Pat David HaldCLUTs/RawTherapee film sims, Popul-AR/gmic-luts) with 30 complimentary samples and 170 unlockable items for a one-time $2.99 purchase.  
- **Monetization decision**: Single unlock replaces any recurring subscription to avoid fatigue; pricing flow spans curation → licensing → bundle pricing → mood/style renaming → publish.  
- **Naming rules**: All LUT names must describe mood or style and explicitly avoid brand references (e.g., Kodak/Fuji) to reduce IP risk.
